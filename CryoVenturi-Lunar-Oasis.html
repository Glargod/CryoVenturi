CryoVenturi Lunar Oasis™

Biodome-Integrated Closed-Loop Cryogenic Plume Recovery + ISRU Agriculture for Sustainable Polar Lunar Habitats

From Compute Waste Heat to Thriving Ecosystem: Power, Water, Food, and Habitat in One Synergistic Loop

Overview & Vision

The CryoVenturi Lunar Oasis extends the original CryoVenturi concept (closed-loop cryogenic plume recovery) to polar lunar outposts. Waste heat plumes from AI/compute clusters drive cryogenic condensation, power recovery, and vapor injection into a pressurized biodome. This creates a regenerative habitat: recovered water + volatiles humidify and heat the atmosphere, enabling agriculture for food, O₂, and CO₂ cycling. On-site 3D printing with regolith turns dust from hazard to resource for repairs and expansion. Bootstrapped by 10–100 kW initial power (rim solar/RTG), the system snowballs to MW-scale self-sufficiency.

Key Goals: 95%+ ECLSS closure, 30–50% power from compute waste, fresh crops for crew nutrition/psychological flourishing, dust mitigation via enclosure, and scalable ISRU for Artemis-era bases near south pole PSRs (water ice access).

Core System Components

  • Compute Cluster (Habitat Core): Generates hot waste plumes (~50–100°C) as primary energy driver.
  • CryoVenturi Ejector: Injects minimal cryogenic fluid (H₂O vapor or H₂ from ice electrolysis) → adiabatic expansion + mixing → 80–95% condensation of plume water/volatiles.
  • PSR Natural Cryo Sink: Ultra-cold regolith (<50–100 K) in shadowed craters for passive condensation/radiator mass reduction (up to 80–90%).
  • CHP Recovery (SOFC/Microturbine): Separated H₂/CH₄ → power (1–5 MW recoverable) + heat/steam for biodome.
  • Pressurized Biodome: Inflatable/rigid enclosure with regolith overburden → warmed/humidified atmosphere (15–25°C, 40–70% RH) for hydroponic/aeroponic crops.
  • ISRU 3D Printing: Regolith-polymer/sintered bricks for dome struts, repairs, mining tools, and expansion (microwave/solar sintering or extrusion).

How It Works: Step-by-Step Loop

  1. Plume Generation: Lunar AI/data center waste heat plume piped to Venturi near polar rim.
  2. Cryo Injection & Condensation: PSR-mined ice → electrolysis → H₂/O₂; inject vapor → adiabatic drop + PSR cold sink → condense water/volatiles (95%+ recovery).
  3. Separation & Power Recovery: Liquid H₂O + gases → electrolysis/CHP → electricity back to compute + steam/heat routed to biodome.
  4. Biodome Injection: Warm vapors + CO₂ traces heat/humidify interior → enable agriculture (e.g., Brassica rapa, duckweed, Arabidopsis for O₂/food).
  5. Crop Cycle & Recycling: Plants photosynthesize → produce O₂/food → transpire water → condense/reuse → waste organics to bio-digesters/Sabatier if needed.
  6. Maintenance & Expansion: 3D print replacements/structures from regolith → minimize resupply; AI optimizes via thermal/CQT momentum feedback.
  7. Self-Accelerating Bootstrap: Recovered power → larger compute → more heat → deeper ice mining → more cryo fluid → bigger biodome → flourishing habitat.
